Welcome to Reflect AI! This guide will walk you through creating your first journal entry and understanding how the app helps you reflect on your thoughts.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Calendar">
    When you launch Reflect AI, you'll see a calendar view showing the current month. Days with existing entries have a small colored dot and mood emoji.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a Date">
    Click on any day to open the journal editor for that date. You can write entries for today, or reflect on past days.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Your Approach">
    Reflect AI offers two ways to start writing:
    
    **Option 1: Quick Nudges (Recommended for beginners)**
    - For new entries, you'll see the Nudges section
    - Type quick notes about your day (e.g., "morning run", "stressful meeting", "dinner with friends")
    - Press Enter or click "Add Nudge" to save each note
    - Once you have 2-3 nudges, click "Generate Entry from Nudges"
    - The AI will create a full journal entry from your notes
    
    **Option 2: Free Writing**
    - Click "Skip, I'll write myself" to access the text editor directly
    - Start typing your thoughts freely
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use Writing Prompts">
    At the top of the editor, you'll see prompt chips:
    - **Free**: Open-ended writing
    - **Gratitude**: "Today I'm grateful for..."
    - **Reflection**: "Today's highlights... What went well... What I learned..."
    - **Goals**: "My intentions..."
    - **Emotions**: "Right now I'm feeling..."
    
    Click any prompt to change the placeholder text and guide your writing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Write Your Entry">
    Type your thoughts in the text area. The editor automatically:
    - Counts your words (shown in the header)
    - Saves your draft as you type
    - Provides a distraction-free writing space
    
    <Tip>
      There's no "right" way to journal. Write as much or as little as you feel comfortable with. Even 50 words can capture meaningful moments.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Context (Optional)">
    Below the text area, you can:
    - **Add Tags**: Click "+ tag" to categorize your entry (work, personal, health, etc.)
    - **Attach Photos**: Click "+ Photo" to add up to 10 images (memories, screenshots, etc.)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Your Entry">
    Click the **Save** button in the top-right corner. Within seconds:
    - Your entry is saved securely to your device
    - The app analyzes the sentiment of your writing
    - You'll see a mood emoji appear (😊 🙂 😐 😔 😢)
    - You may receive an encouraging message based on your mood
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Mood Detection

After saving, Reflect AI automatically analyzes your entry using VADER sentiment analysis:

- **😊 Very Positive** (Joyful): Compound score ≥ 0.5
- **🙂 Positive** (Content): Compound score 0.2 to 0.5
- **😐 Neutral** (Balanced): Compound score -0.2 to 0.2
- **😔 Negative** (Reflective): Compound score -0.5 to -0.2
- **😢 Very Negative** (Processing): Compound score ≤ -0.5

The mood detection is based on the overall emotional tone of your words, not a judgment. All emotions are valid parts of the human experience.

<Note>
  Mood detection happens only when you save. While editing, the mood indicator won't update until you click Save again.
</Note>

## Tips for Meaningful Journaling

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Write consistently, not perfectly">
    Focus on building the habit rather than writing "perfect" entries. Even short entries on busy days help maintain your practice.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Be honest with yourself">
    Your journal is private and stays on your device. Write truthfully about your feelings without self-censorship.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Include specific details">
    Instead of "Had a good day," try "Felt energized after my morning walk and the positive feedback from my manager."
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Note patterns over time">
    Mention recurring activities (exercise, sleep quality, social time) to help Reflect AI identify patterns in future insights.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use voice input for convenience">
    Click the microphone icon to dictate your entry. This is great for capturing thoughts on the go.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Editing and Deleting Entries

**To Edit an Entry:**
1. Click the date on the calendar
2. Make your changes in the text editor
3. Click Save to update

**To Delete an Entry:**
1. Open the entry you want to remove
2. Click the **Delete** button in the top-right
3. Confirm the deletion (this cannot be undone)

<Warning>
  Deleting an entry permanently removes it from your journal. Make sure to export your data regularly as a backup.
</Warning>

## Unsaved Changes Protection

Reflect AI automatically detects unsaved changes:
- If you try to close an entry with unsaved edits, you'll see a confirmation dialog
- Choose "Leave" to discard changes or "Stay" to continue editing
- This prevents accidental data loss
